What Happens Next?

After you upload your term sheet document, our system automatically processes it through several stages:

1

AI Extraction

Our advanced AI technology extracts all relevant data points from your document, regardless of format or structure.

2

Validation

The system cross-checks extracted data against industry standards and your previous documents to identify inconsistencies.

3

Risk Scoring

A comprehensive fraud risk assessment is performed, providing you with detailed scoring and potential red flags.

4

Notifications

You'll receive email alerts with validation results and recommendations for improving your term sheet.
